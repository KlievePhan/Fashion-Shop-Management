Returns & Exchanges

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags attached. Sale items and final sale products are not eligible for return.

To initiate a return or exchange, log into your account and go to "Order History." Select the order you wish to return, click "Return Items," and follow the prompts. You'll receive a prepaid return label via email.

Once we receive your return, please allow 5-7 business days for processing. Your refund will be issued to the original payment method and may take an additional 3-5 business days to appear in your account.

Yes! You can exchange items for a different size or color. Simply initiate a return and select "Exchange" as your reason. We'll send you the new item as soon as we receive your return.

Ordering & Payment

We accept Visa, Mastercard, AMEX, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Velora gift cards.

Yes, you can apply promo codes and gift cards at checkout. Enter your code in the designated field on the payment page. Please note that only one promo code can be used per order.

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, the order enters our fulfillment process and cannot be changed. Please contact customer service immediately if you need assistance.

Yes, select items are available for pre-order. Pre-order items will show an estimated ship date on the product page. Your payment will be processed at the time of order, and items will ship as soon as they become available.

Shipping & Delivery

We currently ship to all 50 US states, Canada, UK, Australia, and select countries in Europe and Asia. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination.

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 business days) and overnight shipping are also available at checkout. International orders typically arrive within 10-15 business days.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history.

If your package hasn't arrived within the expected timeframe, please contact our customer service team. We'll work with the carrier to locate your package or arrange for a replacement or refund.

Sizing & Fit

Each product page includes a detailed size guide with measurements. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing your measurements to our size chart for the best fit.

Most of our items run true to size. If an item runs small or large, we'll note this in the product description. Customer reviews also often include helpful sizing information.

If you're between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit. Check the product description for fit details (slim, regular, relaxed) to help guide your decision.
